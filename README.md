# Power BI Mobile Sales Dashboard

This project is an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes mobile phone sales performance across India. It is part of my data analytics portfolio and focuses on turning raw sales data into clear business insights.

## Project Overview

The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand:

- Total sales, quantity sold, number of transactions, and average order value  
- Which cities, brands, and mobile models are performing best  
- How customers are rating their purchases  
- Which payment methods are most popular  
- Monthly and weekday sales trends over time  

The goal is to support data‑driven decisions such as marketing focus, inventory planning, and product strategy.

## Key Features

- Map of total sales by city across India  
- Line chart of total quantity by year and month  
- Table of sales by brand with total sales and quantity  
- Bar chart of customer ratings distribution  
- Pie chart of transactions by payment method (UPI, debit card, credit card, cash)  
- Bar chart of total sales by mobile model  
- Sales by day name to identify peak days in the week  

All visuals are fully interactive and connected through slicers for brand, payment method, mobile model, and day of week.

## Tech Stack

- Power BI Desktop  
- Power Query for data cleaning and transformation  
- DAX for calculated measures and KPIs  
- CSV/Excel sales data as the data source  

These tools are commonly used in real-world business intelligence projects for building end‑to‑end dashboards.

## How to Use

1. Download `BI SALES DASHBOARD.pbix` from this repository.  
2. Open it in Power BI Desktop.  
3. Explore the dashboard using the slicers to filter by brand, payment method, mobile model, and day name.  
4. Modify the data source to connect your own sales data if you want to reuse the design.
